Uber Analyzer Options

This application goes through your Uber Driver Data and displays it to you in a more friendly way so you can better understand how much you're making, how much Uber is taking from you on each trip, and when/where it is best to drive for Uber.

Please read this document: Document

Please uninstall this application if you do not agree to its terms.

By using this extension, you are granting this extension access to your Uber Driver Data

Instructions:

1) Go to another tab and make sure you are logged in to https://partners.uber.com

2) Click the black UA Icon in the top right hand corner and login to your google account. This is used as your login into the application.

After you have completed these two steps the application will start getting your data and it will all be displayed on https://uber-analyzer-view.herokuapp.com/

Please keep your computer open for at least 10 minutes to allow the transfer of data to take place
